# ScreenAurora

## Purpose

The soft brand **"aurora" band** used behind layered screen headers — the booking-funnel steps and the
Profile hero. A paper base with two diffuse brand glows that the rounded content card sits over.

## Usage

- Drop it into `PageShell`'s `band` slot: `band={<ScreenAurora />}`.
- It fills its parent (`flex: 1`); pass `style` to tweak.

<Canvas of={ScreenAuroraStories.Default} />

## Do not use

- ❌ As a general gradient/glow primitive — that's `RadialGlow` (this is the specific branded band).

## Accessibility

Purely decorative background; carries no semantics.

## Tokens used

`background.paper` (base) · `background.brandDefault` (glows). Composes `RadialGlow`.
